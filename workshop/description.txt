[h1]Stress Schedules[/h1]
[b]Current version: 2.1.1[/b]

Automatically gives stressed duplicants more downtime and restores their original schedule when they recover.

[b]Features[/b]
[list]
[*]Creates a [b]Mild-Stressed[/b] schedule with grouped work and downtime blocks.
[*]Creates a [b]Stressed[/b] schedule that replaces work blocks with downtime.
[*]Moves each duplicant between schedules according to their stress level.
[*]Immediately forces duplicants at 40% health or below onto Stressed until they recover to 60%.
[*]Health recovery takes priority over stress and uses its own hysteresis.
[*]Uses separate entry and recovery thresholds to avoid rapid schedule switching.
[*]Restores each duplicant's previous schedule after recovery.
[*]Supports both standard and Bionic duplicants.
[*]Safely updates assignments while the Schedules screen is open.
[*]Thresholds can be customized from the in-game [b]Options[/b] button.
[*]Option changes apply immediately and persist across local/Workshop updates.
[/list]

[b]Default thresholds[/b]
[list]
[*]Normal → Mild-Stressed: 35%
[*]Mild-Stressed → Stressed: 60%
[*]Stressed → Mild-Stressed: below 45%
[*]Mild-Stressed → original schedule: below 20%
[*]Any schedule → Stressed: health at or below 40%
[*]Leave forced health recovery: health at or above 60%
[/list]
